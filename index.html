Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 17, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how the Hummid Weather App (“we”, “our”, or “us”) collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our application.

1. Information We Collect

Hummid requires access to certain data to provide accurate and location-based weather information:

  • Location Data: We collect your device’s location (approximate and precise) to provide weather forecasts for your current area.
  • Internet Access: We connect to weather APIs over the internet to retrieve weather data relevant to your location.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your location is used exclusively to fetch local weather data. We do not store, share, or track your location after the app is closed. No personal identifiers are collected.

3. Permissions Required

  • ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION / ACCESS_COARSE_LOCATION: To obtain your device’s location accurately.
  • INTERNET: To retrieve real-time weather information from external sources.

4. Data Sharing

We do not share your personal or location data with third parties.

5. Data Retention

We do not retain any user data. All information is accessed in real-time and discarded immediately after use.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
