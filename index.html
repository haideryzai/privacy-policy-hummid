Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 14, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how the Hummid Weather App collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our application.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information to provide core functionality and improve your experience:

  • Location Data: Your device’s approximate and precise location to deliver relevant weather forecasts.
  • Device Information: Information such as device type, OS version, and advertising ID may be collected by third-party services like AdMob.
  • Usage Data: Information on how you interact with the app, collected via analytics or advertising SDKs.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide accurate, location-based weather updates.
  • To serve advertisements through Google AdMob (which may include personalized ads, depending on your preferences).
  • To analyze app usage and improve overall functionality.

3. Permissions Required

  • ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION / ACCESS_COARSE_LOCATION: To determine your device’s location for weather forecasts.
  • INTERNET: To retrieve real-time weather information and deliver ads.

4. Third-Party Services

We use third-party services such as:

  • Google AdMob: To serve ads within the app. AdMob may collect and use certain data, including your advertising ID, for ad targeting and performance measurement.

For more information on how Google uses this data, please visit:
https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads

5. Data Sharing

We do not share your personal or location data directly with any third parties. However, third-party services like AdMob may collect and process data as described in their respective policies.

6. Data Retention

We do not store personal data on our servers. Data accessed through the app is used in real-time and not retained by us.

7. Consent and User Control

If you're located in the European Economic Area (EEA), the app may prompt you to provide consent for personalized ads through Google’s User Messaging Platform (UMP). You can update your preferences at any time.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Changes will be posted on this page with a new revision date.
